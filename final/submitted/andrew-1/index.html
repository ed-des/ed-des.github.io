Introduction

The Steam car is making a grand return! Heres your chance to race the fastest car invented. This app will allow you to race any car you want against the steam car. Simply scan any moving vehicle by holding on the capture screen and compare to see if your car won against the steam car. Features in the app include the car's captured speed and specifications. The app also includes specifications of different steam car models to match against your scanned vehicles. This fun and interactive app is only just the start, just wait untill you see the finish line.

Homepage

Get a chance to race the Steam car by using your phone's camera to capture the speed of any nearby cars.Simply touch and hold until the progress bar fills up to capture the speed of the car.

Specifications

After your car is finished scanning, a page of your scanned car's specifications will be listed. This page will inform you the different specifications such as how fast your car was going, its engine type, etc.

Results Screen

Once you finish checking out the cool features of your scanned car , you will now have a chance to race the prestigious steam car. Once you press to see who wins in the race, a list of questions will be asked to show how the steam car is just that much better than your scanned car. There are seven questions that need to be answered, so be prepared!
