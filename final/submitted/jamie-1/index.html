Designing Immersive Experiences
The editorial layout has always been a linear process and a linear way of reading. But with virtual and augmented reality technologies, exploring a spacial narrative is possible. This project explores how editorial design can manifest in a physical space and how the reading process can turn from being something static activity to something interactive.How the reading experience works is by using the phone as a platform.
The article can be accessed through the web where the reader is carried through the article. The phone acts as an augmented reality tool where the reader can scan a modern day vehicle using their mobile device, and visually explore a steam engined vehicle through the screen of their device. Text is layered over these changing visuals and can only be read if the reader hovers the phone in the designated areas.
The reader is free to physically walk around the vehicle to discover and choose which parts of the article they would like to read and experience.This type of reading experience allows the reader to have an experience that is more immersive than a normal reading session. The reader can choose and curate their own experience within structured parameters.
