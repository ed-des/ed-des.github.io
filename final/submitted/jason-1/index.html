Have you ever wanted to travel back in time?

Window in NY Time

An immersive mobile reading experience transporting you to New York City in the 1970s.

Newspaper

Narrative

Window

Experience ten episodes with visual, audio and narrative elements from a year in New York City in the 1970s. Each episode is themed: highlighting a major event from that year. Preview newspaper articles, read a short narrative from a different New Yorker living in the city from that year, and view a vignette of the city related to the specific time of day of the release of the episode.

Reading Mode

Open the app to a new, or the first, episode. You are immediately taken to the Reading Menu page. From here, you can read the narrative chapter from a person living in New York during that year or choose from a selection of newspaper clippings to provide contextual background for the theme of the episode.

The primary narrative shifts perspectives each episode, featuring a different New Yorker's thoughts, opinions and recollections on life in the city during the 1970s.

At the end of the episode's chapter, you will be prompted with a time of day to reopen the app next to receive the next episode in the series. Window in NY Time is serialized in ten episodes; each episode is released at a different time of day, one per day.

From the Reading Menu, access archived New York Times newspaper articles that provide contextual background for the theme of the episode.

Tapping on the newspaper allows you to read a synopsis of the article from the year featured in the episode.

To transition to Window Viewing Mode: shift your arm with an upward gesture, holding your phone parallel to your body.

Window Viewing Mode

Window Viewing Mode provides an immersive visual and auditory experience allowing you to compare sights and sounds from 1970s with a livestream from New York City today. The time of day the episode is released corresponds to the Eastern Pacific Time zone, allowing you to compare a 1970s morning in NY with a morning in the Big Apple today.

Transition between viewing and reading modes through a simple gesture. Window in NY Time responds intuitively to accomodate the position you hold your device.

Window Viewing Mode will transform your screen into a portal, immersing you into the sights and sounds of New York City in the 1970s.

Reading Mode provides an engaging, serialized narrative that will keep you coming back for more.
