Title This.

Ever pick up a book just because of its compelling title? Ever got through a book pleased but shocked by how undeveloped and unappealing its title is in comparison? Ever wondered why?

Contrary to the popular belief, book titles have their own story of being created, edited and chosen. With Title This., you can get the fun facts about the book titles as well as create and submit your own!

How It Works

It's easy. All you need is your book and your phone. Turn to the title page of your book. Open the app to scan the title page. Once you do, the app will generate the relevant information that you can listen to.

Other Features

While you listen to the podcast about your scanned book title, you can peruse other features on the app.

Check out title pages and podcasts of titles with similar context or from the same time period.

If you like what you see and hear, you can save by bookmarking.

Got a better title? Submit yours and check out titles created by other users.
