SUMMER IN NY

How cool kids spend their summer.

Seek for exciting free adventures in NY!

Want to know what cool kids doing during summer?
Then, follow me!

1. Tell us your name!

2. Choose and start playing games!

Simply by clicking an icon, you can play a game.

3. Add your favorite things to your wishlist.

Your wishlist will be refreshed every two days.

4.Go to your parents and show them what you like!

The information section shows the places that will be open today and tomorrow. If your parents click an image, they will get more specific information about that place.

Now, enjoy your summer!

This webpage is designed for the course, Editorial Design for Screen instructed by Dylan Greif. We are asked to choose one of the publication articles, reimagine it as a serialized story either over time or space, then design a new way for the reader of tomorrow to access and read it.
