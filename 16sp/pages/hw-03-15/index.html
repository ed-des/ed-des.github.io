Assignment from 03-15

Articles w/ Personality

Pick an an interview from The Great Discontent. Redesign the article, inspired by the personality of the interviewee.

Consider the design of different elements of the article’s anatomy.

Just design the article elements. Do not include the website’s logo, navigation, share buttons, related articles, or any content outside of the article itself.

You can use the artwork in the article, or you can create your own. Whatever serves your design direction.

This will be a two-week long assignment. We will do group reviews next week, so you can submit a final version the week after. Therefore, for next week, I encourage you to sketch any ideas you ended up having too much technical difficulty with, so we can find solutions next week.

Submit: Submit your working folder containing your html and css files. Label the html file index.html and the folder firstname-1. Upload the folder to our shared google drive folder “Assignments” by 11:00AM next Tuesday.
