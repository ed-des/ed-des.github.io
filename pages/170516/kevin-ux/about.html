ABOUT

OPEN DOOR is an application function that connects your phone interface to your car. In addition to the already existing function of connecting your phone to your car to talk, OPEN DOOR allows our windows to be an extension of our phone screen. OPEN DOOR allows families and friends to have a shared experience while driving to their destination. You can connect apps on your phone to your car window interface and watch netflix, read interactive news, or FaceTime.

Created by Kevin Kunwoo Kim, RISD/GD Spring 2017, Dylan Grief
