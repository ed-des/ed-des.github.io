The project is a breaking news "letter" and design that showcases an important, major, breaking news article.



It is for people all over the world who need to be informed.

The user will recieve this information once the "letter" reaches their mailbox at their home in the morning right when they're drinking their morning coffee and starting off their day.

The user experience should feel shocking and sudden, yet informative. The idea is that when people who find this out will right away want to spread the word and see what other people are talking about. The folds of the project are simple, and can easily be a poster or a small pamphlett in terms of physicallity.
