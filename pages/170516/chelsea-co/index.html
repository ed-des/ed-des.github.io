WEST PALM BEACH, April. Donald Trump, the 45th President of the United States was shot multiple times and killed outside of his Mar-a-Lago mansion in Florida today. Trump died after the assassins shot him from above, in a helicopter and wounded his son-in-law and senior adviser, Jared Kushner, after he was shot in the shoulder. Trump and Kushner had been playing golf on a sunny Florida day before the tragedy happened.




Vice President, Mike Pence, who was back in the White House, was sworn in as the 46th President of the United States once Trump was confirmed dead.

Trump is the fifth President to be assassinated in office since John F. Kennedy. The assassins have yet to be identified and the U.S government is offering a reward of $1 million dollars to anyone who can anonymously give them a correct lead to the assassins.
