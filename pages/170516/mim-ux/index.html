tapped

tales,

a smart card.

All stories on the smart card are personal experiences written by users. They can then share these personal stories with their community.

The ID sized smart card is small and travel-friendly, able to fit in your wallet or phone holder. Share your own stories or read stories received from friends.

navigation

Swipe down to reveal the hidden navigation bar.
Use the bar to browse Collection, Popular, or Profile. Swipe up to hide the bar.

popular

View daily popular stories to see the top five most tapped stories of the day. Collect the story to see it appear on the popular list.

collect

Collect and share stories by tapping your smart card with your friends. Sort your collection of stories by date or by taps.

share

and

receive

Tap your card with your friend's card to share and receive stories.
