Swipe up and down to cutomize your view setting and choose your favorite news category (Breaking News, Music, Health, Lifestyle, Sports, Entertainment) to begin

After having to select a category, a digital sleeve will detect the temperature of the cup and the hottest topics will start to view in an order. Hotter the liquid, hotter the topics are and cooler the liquid, more mundane the topics are









9AM

After picking what kind of news you want to checkout, thumbnails of the news start to appear with the bubbles. Each colors of the bubbles are designated according to the news article you've chosen. The bubbles show how many people are viewing the same article at the same time. More the bubbles, more the people viewing the article on live with you





3PM

Bubbles start to disappear as the number of people viewing the news are shrinking





6PM

Bubbles start to reappear and grow as the number of people viewing the news are increasing



Time Limit

There are only 10 seconds to view the article thumbnail and the time is shown on the top right corner of the screen


Link to your phone

If you wish to continue to read the article, hold down on to the icon on top right corner. It will automatically link your bubble with the phone and will be able to view the whole article
