Design for Journalists

Type Compositions (Assigned 2/4)

Extract one concept from your home work last week (attention, delight, narrative, clarity...). Design five different 8" x 8" compositions inspired by that concept. All should be in black and white. All should use just one typeface (but you may use different weights and styles). This is a formal exercise, and your chance to play with:

  • Tone
  • Continuity
  • Contrast
  • Modulation
  • Undulation
  • Negative Space
  • Abstraction
  • Balance
  • Texture
  • Movement

Use Typekit to find your font, and complete this project in Illustrator. Print your pages out (with trim marks). Then save your work as a 5-page PDF. Name your PDF your first name, all lowercase (name.pdf). Upload the file to our shared google drive “Assignments” folder by 11:00AM next Thursday.
